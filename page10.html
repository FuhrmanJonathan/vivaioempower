Lessons

Lessons can be planned weekly or bi-weekly at around 11 PM PST. Schools can choose what lessons they want to be taught, deciding individually for each lesson. In addition, lessons can be arranged from 45 minutes to 1.5 hours, and homework can be decided by the school, whether or not it is given to the kids.

Careers

Introduces children to various professions from different countries, showcasing diverse career paths and the skills needed for success. Each session features guest speakers who are professionals in medicine, engineering, art, and business, sharing their experiences and insights.  

Geography and World Culture

World Culture consists on lessons of multiple different countries and regions from around the world. Kids learn about entertainment, language, landscape, and animals from the particular region.

Health

It focuses on promoting medical knowledge and hygiene practices among school children. Students learn about essential health topics such as primary medical care, hygiene routines, disease prevention, and the importance of vaccinations. Guest speakers, including local healthcare professionals, provide insights into healthcare challenges and solutions relevant to their region. 

A Day in My Life

A day in my life is a once-a-month connection between one of our schools in Vivaio Empower and one of the elementary schools here in the U.S. The class is essentially a connection between the two different schools, done to show both schools what life is like 9,400 miles away. For more information, please contact us!

Public Speaking and Presentation Skills

Aims to empower school children with practical communication skills and confidence in expressing their ideas. Students learn the fundamentals of public speaking, including speech structure, body language, and vocal delivery. They explore various speaking styles and techniques through interactive activities such as impromptu speeches, storytelling, and debates, all sparking creativity in the students. 

Science and Technology

Educates children about the evolution of technology and its profound effects on society. Each slideshow provides an engaging overview of various technological advancements using images and videos, from early inventions to cutting-edge developments.
