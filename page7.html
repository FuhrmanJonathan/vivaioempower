Greenfield School
Kabale, Uganda

Greenfield School is a primary school in the Kabale region of Uganda. The school has a population of 500 students from K-6, and ran by 19 teachers. The school provides meal and board if necessary, where 35% of the school stays at the school during the school year due to living too far from the school. The rest of the students walk anywhere from 30 min to 3 hours to get to school and back home everyday. These students work hard learning English, Math, and Ugandan History. Students attend school from 8:30 am - 2 pm.

Greenfield was the test school for the project, by being able to have sessions with the students we were able to see the potential that our project hold as a whole. Through Greenfield we were able to test the success rate of our lessons and pick and choose the best methods for transferring the information over to the students.
