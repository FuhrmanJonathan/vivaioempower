Arise & Shine Primary School
Pallisa, East Uganda

Arise & Shine Primary School, situated in Pallisa, East Uganda, embodies a spirit of hope and opportunity through education for the local children. Likely established as a community-driven or privately run initiative, the school serves as a vital resource in the Pallisa district, aiming to uplift young lives through access to primary education.

Reflecting a similar empowering ethos to VIVAIO EMPOWER, Arise & Shine Primary School strives to be a beacon of positive change within its community. By providing a learning environment, the school equips children with foundational knowledge and skills, fostering their potential to rise and shine. This dedication to education empowers young individuals to build brighter futures for themselves and contribute to the overall well-being of their community in Eastern Uganda.
