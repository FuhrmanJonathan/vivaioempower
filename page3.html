Hello, I am Jonny Fuhrman, a 17-year-old Junior in the Los Angeles area high school. My passion for learning began when I learned how to code, sparking a passion to share this excitement with others. This inspiration led me to seek roles, volunteering in my community and teaching students math, programming, and reading skills.

Through these experiences, I realized the profound impact that education can have on young minds. Together with the rest of the team, we are dedicated to teaching youth worldwide about the excitement of learning. I aim to inspire these students to further education and develop a broader understanding of culture and the world. I believe that through education, we can empower these young individuals to fulfill their dreams and create a brighter future.
